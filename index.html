Biography

Hanbin Hong is a 3rd-year Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science and Engineering at University of Connecticut, advised by Prof. Yuan Hong. His research interests lie in security and privacy issues in Machine Learning, especially in the development of innovative, universal, and efficient adversarial attacks and defense for ML models. He is currently working on developing more universal and practical certifiable robustness schemes for ML.
